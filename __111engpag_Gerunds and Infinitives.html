Gerunds and Infinitives

f t g+ p

To use gerunds or to use infinitives, that is the question! Learning how to use gerunds and infinitives is one of the most challenging aspects of learning English. For this reason, Englishpage.com has created the Internet's most in-depth tutorial on the subject. Click here to begin our three-part tutorial. Or if you prefer, you can click on one of the gerunds and infinitives exercises below and test what you already know.
